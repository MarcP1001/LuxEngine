# Deployment Comparison: Hostinger KVM2 VPS vs. Railway

This guide compares deploying LuxEngine on a **Hostinger KVM2 VPS** versus **Railway** to help you choose the best fit for your workflow.

---

## 1. Hostinger KVM2 VPS (Highly Recommended for Control & Persistence)

A **KVM2 VPS** (2 vCPUs, 4GB RAM, NVMe storage) is an **excellent, high-performance home for LuxEngine**. 

### Why the VPS is a Great Fit:
- **100% Persistent Storage:** Crucial for keeping your Claude CLI workspace files (`./agent_workspace`) and messaging bot states completely active and saved forever.
- **Infinite Execution Time:** Absolutely zero timeouts. Ideal for long LLM streaming calls.
- **Fully Leverages Your Deployment Files:** You can instantly use the Systemd template (`deploy/free-claude-code.service`) and the Nginx/Caddy reverse-proxy configs (`deploy/reverse-proxy.md`) we already built!
- **Fixed Monthly Cost:** You pay a flat rate to Hostinger without worrying about usage-based scaling costs.

### Setting it up on Hostinger VPS:
1. Choose Ubuntu 22.04 or 24.04 as your OS.
2. Clone your repository to the server.
3. Install dependencies or run via Docker using our `Dockerfile`.
4. Set up the Systemd service (`deploy/free-claude-code.service`) to keep the proxy running on boot.
5. Set up Nginx/Caddy (`deploy/reverse-proxy.md`) for automatic SSL (HTTPS).

---

## 2. Railway (Highly Recommended for Convenience & Zero-Ops)

If you do not want to manage SSH, SSL certs, firewalls, or Nginx configurations, **Railway** provides an incredibly smooth experience.

### Which Railway Plan to Choose?
If deploying on Railway, you **must use the Developer Plan** ($5/month credit-based model + actual usage).
- **Why?** The Hobby/Free tiers automatically sleep your container after inactivity, which will **turn off your Discord/Telegram bots**. The Developer Plan guarantees 24/7 uptime so your remote workers are always awake.

### Why Railway is Great:
- **One-click Deploy:** Links to your GitHub repo, detects our `Dockerfile`, and deploys it automatically.
- **Zero Ops:** Automatic HTTPS (SSL) is provisioned instantly without Nginx configurations.

### Limitations:
- **Ephemeral Disks:** Containers are ephemeral. If your container redeploys or restarts, any files generated by Claude inside `./agent_workspace` will be wiped out unless you attach a **Railway Volume** to the service under project settings.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
